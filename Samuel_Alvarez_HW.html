  1. Compute the inter and intra-class variance of quantity according to cartel. Verify the relation of the decompostion of variance.
In [52]:
n_total = len(df)
global_mean = df['quantity'].mean()
global_var = df['quantity'].var(ddof=0)

we get the main of mean and variance, alongside the amount in each category

In [53]:
group_stats = df.groupby('cartel')['quantity'].agg(
    n_k='count',
    mean_k='mean',
    var_k=lambda x: x.var(ddof=0)
).reset_index()

print(group_stats)

Here we retrieve the values of each category, the mean and variance, to compute the inter and intra class variance
